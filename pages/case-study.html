Font & Dingbat Design

Lacrimosa is the name of the first font I’ve created. This font is based off of my most commonly used typefaces, condensed sans-serifs and slab serifs. I love the edge and drama blackletter fonts have and the minimalist, clean look of sans serifs, so the goal was to create a combination of those features. The traditional tattoo dingbats compliment the font’s modern edge.
